The Perfect PB&J

Description

An alien once asked how to how to make the perfect PB&J. These were the simple steps I spoke.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat bread (this is why)
  • All nutrual peanut butter
  • Fresh french preseve grape jelly (1989 or older)

Directions

  1. Take out bread
  2. Apply peanut butter to the face of one side.
  3. Set down the piece to focus on another.
  4. Ya'll know we puttin' jelly on this one. NOTE: too much jelly is the number one reason for leaky drips. Spread carefully.
  5. Slowly bring the two slices of bread together (jelly side facing peanut side), careful to aline edges.
